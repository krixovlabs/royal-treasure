Our Heritage

A Dynasty of
Curators & Collectors

"Every object that passes through our doors carries the breath of history and the touch of royalty."

Founded in 1978 by Shri Rajendra Singh Rathore, Royal Treasure was born from a lifelong devotion to preserving the material heritage of Rajputana. What began as a single shop in the walled city of Jaipur has grown into one of India's most respected antique galleries — visited by collectors, curators, and tourists from across the globe.

Every piece is rigorously authenticated by our panel of historians and conservation experts. We source directly from ancestral estates, palace archives, and noble families who entrust us with their most prized heirlooms. Whether you are a first-time visitor to the Pink City or a seasoned connoisseur, we welcome you into a world of heritage.

Vikram Singh Rathore Third Generation Proprietor

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying antique items, heritage collectibles, and royal artefacts in India

Where can I buy authentic antique items in India?

Royal Treasure in Jaipur, Rajasthan is one of India's most respected antique emporiums, established in 1978. We offer over 3,200 authenticated artefacts including Mughal paintings, Rajput arms, heritage jewellery, antique furniture, and royal collectibles. Visit our showroom at Tripolia Bazar in Jaipur's Old City, or enquire online to buy antique items from anywhere in India and the world.

How can I buy heritage property or antique pieces in Rajasthan?

Royal Treasure offers a curated collection of heritage items from Rajasthan's noble families, palace estates, and royal trusts. Each piece comes with authentication certificates and provenance documentation. We offer private acquisition services, valuations, and can help you acquire heritage properties and antique pieces across Rajasthan and India.

Are Royal Treasure's antiques authenticated and genuine?

Yes, every piece at Royal Treasure is rigorously authenticated by our panel of historians and conservation experts. We provide certificates of provenance and detailed documentation for all artefacts. We source directly from ancestral estates and noble families to guarantee authenticity of every antique item you buy.

Does Royal Treasure ship antique items internationally from India?

Yes, we offer white-glove global shipping with specialist packing and full insurance. We have served collectors in over 47 countries. Our team handles all customs documentation and export permits required for shipping antique and heritage items from India internationally.

What types of royal items can I buy at Royal Treasure, Jaipur?

Our collection of royal and heritage items includes Mughal miniature paintings, Rajput arms and armour (swords, daggers, shields), Meenakari and Kundan heritage jewellery, antique royal furniture (thrones, cabinets), Jaipuri block-print textiles, Bidriware metalwork, heritage sculptures, Jaipur blue pottery, silver palanquins, and antique Jharokha frames.

Is Royal Treasure the best antique shop in Jaipur, Rajasthan?

Royal Treasure has been Jaipur's premier antique emporium since 1978, now proudly run by the third generation of the Rathore family. With over 3,200 rare artefacts, 46 years of heritage expertise, and collectors from 47 countries, we are one of India's most trusted names in the antique, heritage, and royal collectibles market.

How are antique items priced at Royal Treasure in India?

Prices for antique items depend on age, rarity, provenance, condition, and historical significance. Our collection ranges from ₹1,85,000 for heritage metalwork pieces to ₹48,50,000 for exceptional royal furniture. We also offer independent valuation services for insurance, estate planning, or understanding the worth of inherited treasures.

Can I visit Royal Treasure's antique emporium in Jaipur, Rajasthan?

Yes! We welcome visitors at our showroom at 4, Gangauri Bazaar, Tripolia Bazar, Old City, Jaipur 302002, Rajasthan. We are open Monday to Saturday from 10:00 AM to 7:30 PM. Private tours and Sunday visits are available by appointment. We are just 20 minutes from Jaipur International Airport with chauffeur transfers available.
